Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does the ActiveCampaign integration work?

Connect your ActiveCampaign account to OOPSpam, pull your lists into the dashboard, scan contacts for spammy, risky and suspicious signals, then review the flagged results.

Do I need to export contacts from ActiveCampaign?

No. The workflow is designed around a direct API connection, so you can review contacts inside OOPSpam without managing CSV exports.

What can I do with flagged contacts?

After a scan, you can review flagged contacts and choose whether to unsubscribe them from the list or delete the contact completely.
